Facilitator Volunteer

Release the Fear is always seeking qualified facilitators who feel passionate about making a difference in the lives of disadvantaged and at-risk youth. Applicants must be skilled and confident in providing positive interactive presentations to teenagers. Candidates will be trained through art, music and communication to teach Character Education and establish tools for youth to develop better cognitive behavioral skills. To apply, please fill out the facilitator application and return it with your resume in one email with attachments to sara@releasethefear.org.
