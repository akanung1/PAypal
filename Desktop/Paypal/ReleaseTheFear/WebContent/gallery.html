Enjoy a masterpiece of expression of hope, love and understanding.

At the end of the 3-day workshop, as the culmination and pinnacle of their experiences drawn from the program, participants create a group painting painted by between 20 to 30 youth together, to present to the public; an expression created by persons from multiple perspectives can be harmonious and unifying.

A tangible reminder of what beauty they can accomplish when they put their mind to it.

Participants understand that this is their way to support others like themselves to experience the Release the Fear workshop.

In other Words, their way of paying it forward and provides another opportunity for you to see their dreams come true.
Please visit Release the Fear’s new space at Grace Chapel, 302 W. Monroe, in the downtown Phoenix Arts District.
